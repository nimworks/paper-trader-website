Supported Brokers

Kaagzi requires you to login to any of the supported brokers in order for it to create the mock trading environment using real-time live data for you to practice taking dummy positions in. Kaagzi supports Angel One, 5paisa and Zerodha brokers.


Choose any of the following brokers

Open account with Angel OneOpen account with 5paisaOpen account with Zerodha

Signing into broker

Once you login into Kaagzi, you shall get an option to choose any of the supported brokers you wish to sign into. You simply need to choose the broker of your choice and click the login button if you are not already logged in with the broker. Upon clicking the login button, you shall see a signin dialog page presented by the broker.
